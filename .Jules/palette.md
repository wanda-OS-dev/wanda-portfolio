## 2024-05-24 - Form Input Accessibility & Feedback
**Learning:** Adding `autoComplete` attributes to standard form fields improves user experience and accessibility, while applying `disabled` states and appropriate styling (like `disabled:opacity-50 disabled:cursor-not-allowed`) to inputs and textareas during form submission prevents multiple submissions and provides clear visual feedback of the active process.
**Action:** Always include `autoComplete` attributes on relevant form fields and ensure inputs/textareas are explicitly disabled during asynchronous operations.
